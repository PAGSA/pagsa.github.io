About PAGSA

PAGSA is an established academic unit, formed to enhance the university experience of students within the Department of Physics and Astronomy at the University of Victoria.

Our mission

Our mission is to support graduate student success & well–being. We do this by providing academic resources, promoting an inclusive social community, and advocating on their behalf.
