About the club

OrbitClub started as a small group of students who wanted a space to build things together, outside of coursework and deadlines. Today we're a growing community that meets weekly to learn, tinker, and support each other's projects.

Our mission

Give students a low-pressure, high-curiosity place to try new things — whether that's a first line of code, a first public talk, or a first leadership role.
